About the Project

The Name itself clarifys the motto of company is Swachhta, but in the view of the company people are already motivated for Swachhta but they need regular repetitive awareness therefore company has designed a Swachhta Kit, which consists of Dust Bin Bags for Cars, Mobile Dust Bin Which they can Carry and Pan Dan in which they can Spit above all stickers in car that they Pledged for Swachhta and motivates other to follow. Company has designed a portable machine to convert wet Garbage into manure.

Being Highly Motivated from our Respected Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modiji, We think it is our moral responsibility to lead in Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and transform our Country in to the world’s best Country.

Because being in Marketing field for so many years repetitive awareness is must for any such big Abhiyan, We also want to be a part and contribute for our Country.
